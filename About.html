Who are we?

We are The Physics Club from Don Bosco Institute of Technology, Mumbai. Finding simple answers to complex and twisted questions. Unwrapping the principles and constantly challenging ourselves to explore the world of physics. From talks to fun experiments and conducting research to work on projects, we enjoy our own ways to make physics more interesting and fun to learn.


About Virtual Lab Project

Virtual Lab Project is a mission to bring engineering physics to life in the virtual world of online learning. Our main objective of this project on Crystallography is to give our learners an experience to visualize and interact with the models. We have attempted to present an immersive view of the world of crystallography.
