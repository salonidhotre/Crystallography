Crystals

Diamond

It is formed by the interpenetration of two FCC sub-lattices along the body diagonal by 1/4th cube edge.
A unit cell has:
1. Eight corner atoms
2. Six face centered atoms
3. Four more atoms located inside



ZnS

It has the similar structure as Diamond.
The Eight corner atoms and Six face centered atoms are of Sulphur.
The Four atoms located inside are of Zinc



NaCl

Sodium Chloride is an ionic compund and belongs to the FCC structure.
The small atoms represent Na+ ion and big atoms represent Cl- ion

Hexagonal Closed Packed (HCP)

It has three layers. The top and bottom form a hexagon and middle layer forms a triangle.
